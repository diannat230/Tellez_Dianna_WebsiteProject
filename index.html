About Kasama

Originally located in Newark, New Jersey, Kasama has grown to be the cities most loved Filipino kitchen. You'll find the most traditional dishes and foods you have never even seen before. At Kasama, we are dedicated to continue to provide Michelin star service to all customers. All are welcome through Kasama's doors.
